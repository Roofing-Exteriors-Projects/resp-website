Services

Roofing

Asphalt shingles, metal, rubber, cedar, flat roofing, and commercial systems. We help you compare options so your roof matches your budget, design, and performance needs.

Siding

Vinyl, James Hardie fiber cement, and stucco for new builds and replacements. Proper installation that improves durability, energy efficiency, and curb appeal.

Eavestrough

Continuous eavestrough, downspouts, and related accessories that move water away from your foundation. Options available for leaf protection and ice management.

Windows

Energy efficient replacement windows installed with correct flashing and insulation. Casement, slider, bay, and bow styles available, including triple pane packages for better comfort.

Commercial projects

Roofing and exterior upgrades for retail, office, multifamily, and light industrial buildings. Work is planned around tenants and business hours to reduce disruption.

Repairs and maintenance

Leak investigation, emergency repairs, flashing and valley corrections, and annual maintenance programs that help extend the life of your roof and exterior systems.

Insurance claim support

Storm damage can be stressful. Our team guides you through the insurance process with clear information, organized reports, and steady communication so you always know the next step.

  • On site inspection with photos and video of affected areas
  • Repair or replacement recommendations that follow code and manufacturer guidelines
  • Written scope and estimate formatted to align with insurer requirements
  • Work completed once your claim is approved, with invoices and warranty records for your files
After a storm
  1. Take photos if it is safe to do so
  2. Call us to book a site visit
  3. Contact your insurer to open a claim and note the claim number
  4. We meet on site as needed and finalize the scope with your adjuster
  5. Work is scheduled, completed, and documented with warranty information
